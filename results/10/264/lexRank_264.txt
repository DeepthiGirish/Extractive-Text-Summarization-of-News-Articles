With an election looming the next big speech from Gordon Brown will be closely examined for any signs of divisions and, in particular, the use of that little three letter word.
Under what is to be the general election slogan "Britain is working", the prime minister time and again insisted the future direction would be unremittingly New Labour.
That might get under the skin of Mr Brown, but he also heaped praise on him as the most successful post-war chancellor Britain has had.

Eurosceptic party UKIP have suspended a candidate for allegedly suggesting the criminally insane should be killed. But he was suspended after his reported views, including the return of the British Empire, were sent to two Scottish newspapers. Mr Houston is alleged to have said that the organs of the criminally insane should be "made available to law-abiding members of the community" and proposed the legalisation of drugs and the sex trade.

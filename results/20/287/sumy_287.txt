Betty Boothroyd has said the House of Lords needs its own Speaker and that peers should lead the way on reforming the upper chamber.Baroness Boothroyd, who was the first woman to be Commons Speaker, said she believed Tony Blair initiated reforms without a clear outcome in mind.The lord chancellor hinted further reforms could be unveiled in the next Labour manifesto.Lady Boothroyd said she believed it was unacceptable for the lord chancellor to have the role of Speaker.

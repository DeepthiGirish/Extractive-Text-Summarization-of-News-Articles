The Conservative Party would deal "sympathetically" with any application by disgraced peer Lord Archer to rejoin its ranks, its co-chairman has said.A jury ruled that Lord Archer lied during a libel trial against the Daily Star at the High Court in London in 1987.Meanwhile, senior Conservative MP Sir Teddy Taylor warned that moves bring Lord Archer back into the fold could be controversial.Lord Archer, who was not available for comment, remains a popular figure among constituency Tory parties and is a successful fundraiser.

New European commission president Jose Manuel Barroso has suggested the cash could be shared out among net contributors to the EU budget.He added that he did not think there would be a political price to pay for the UK's stance - Britain contributed more and received less than other EU states.The UK, France, Germany, Austria, the Netherlands and Sweden want the EU budget to be capped at 1% of member states' combined national incomes - the Commission wants it to be 1.26%.
